1.Earth

Earth represents stability, grounding, and the physical foundation of life. It is associated with
soil, rocks, and minerals, symbolizing the material world and our connection to it.


2.Water

Water symbolizes emotions, intuition, and healing. It is present in rivers, oceans, and lakes,
essential for all life forms. Water is often seen as a purifier, capable of washing away impurities
and offering renewal.


3.Fire

Fire represents transformation, energy, and passion. It manifests as flames, heat, and light,
symbolizing both creation and destruction. Fire is a force of change, capable of turning raw
materials into something new.

4.Wind

Wind, or Air, symbolizes intellect, communication, and freedom. It is the breath of life, filling
our lungs and connecting us to the atmosphere. Air represents movement and change, often
seen as a messenger and a carrier of ideas and words.

5.Space

Space, or Ether, represents the spirit, connectivity, and the infinite expanse of the universe.
It is the medium through which the other elements interact, encompassing the sky and
the cosmos. Space symbolizes the unknown and the potential for limitless possibilities.
